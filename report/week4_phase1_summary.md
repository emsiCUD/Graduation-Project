## 6. Bridge to PhoBERT (Phase 2)

- Static embeddings cap out around dev_F1_macro≈0.600. PhoBERT brings contextual sub-word embeddings + 12-layer transformer attention — historical results on ViHSD put it at F1_macro ≈ 0.66-0.69 (Luu et al., 2021).
- Memory profile from 04a E2: `bs=16, max_len=128, fp16=True` fits 6 GB with ~2.8 GB peak, leaving room for the val pass. That's the config 04c will start with.
- Filter rule stays consistent: training-only `min_length=3` (PhoBERT BPE will rarely produce <3 tokens on raw text, but the conservative cut keeps comparison fair across phases).
